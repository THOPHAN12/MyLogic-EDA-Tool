# OpenSpec Template

## Goal

- What outcome is required?

## Scope

- What files, flows, or behaviors are in scope?
- What is explicitly out of scope?

## Assumptions

- What is being assumed to be true?

## Constraints

- What constraints must be preserved?
- Examples: repo-relative paths, existing outputs, compatibility, no destructive changes.

## Plan

1. Inspect the relevant code, scripts, and docs.
2. Decide the smallest safe change.
3. Implement the change.
4. Verify with the most relevant command, demo, or test.

## Verification

- What command or check will prove the change works?
- What evidence will be reported back?
