# Lo Trinh Agentic

Tai lieu nay mo ta flow ma agent can doc truoc khi thuc thi trong repository nay.

## Cac buoc

### Buoc 0: cursor.md

- Doc cac file huong dan va ngu canh truoc khi hanh dong.
- Uu tien `cursor.md`, sau do den `AGENTS.md`, tai lieu trong `docs/`, va cac ghi chu lien quan den task.

### Buoc 1: OpenSpec

- Thong nhat muc tieu, pham vi, va cach lam truoc khi code.
- Voi task khong don gian, phai co tom tat ke hoach ngan gon truoc khi sua.

### Buoc 2: Superpowers

- Lam viec theo quy trinh: inspect -> plan -> implement -> verify.
- Uu tien duong dan tuong doi theo root repo.
- Tranh hard-code duong dan tuyet doi theo may.
- Sau khi sua, chay buoc kiem tra phu hop.

### Buoc 3: Beads

- Giu lai bo nho cua task dai hoi.
- Tai su dung cac quyet dinh, rang buoc, va ket qua da xac nhan trong cuoc hoi thoai.
- Neu co convention moi, dua vao rule hoac tai lieu de lan sau agent doc duoc ngay.

## Trang thai ap dung

- Flow nay da duoc cai thanh rule `alwaysApply` trong `.cursor/rules/agentic-flow.mdc`.
- Muc tieu la de moi lan agent thuc thi trong workspace nay deu uu tien doc va tuan theo flow tren.
- Ban co the dat huong dan nen truc tiep trong `cursor.md` de agent doc som ngay tu dau.
